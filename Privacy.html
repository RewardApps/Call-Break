CallBreak Go: Online Card Game

By installing or using the game, you acknowledge the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Overview

CallBreak Go is a card game that may include online multiplayer, local LAN play, computer opponents, Solitaire, advertisements, rewards, player statistics, and optional in-app purchases.

This policy applies only to CallBreak Go and does not cover websites, apps, or services operated by third parties.

2. Information We Handle

Information you provide

  • Player name, nickname, selected avatar, game preferences, or other profile information entered in the game.
  • Messages or information you send when contacting support.

Information collected automatically

  • Device information such as device model, operating system, language, app version, and general technical identifiers.
  • Gameplay information such as scores, wins, bids, match results, game mode, rewards, and progress.
  • Diagnostic information such as crashes, performance data, errors, and network status.
  • Advertising-related information such as ad interactions, advertising identifiers, and consent choices where applicable.
  • Approximate location inferred from network information where used by advertising or fraud-prevention services.
CallBreak Go does not intentionally request sensitive personal information such as passwords, financial account credentials, government identification numbers, or precise location.

3. How Information Is Used

Information may be used to:

  • Provide and maintain the game's features.
  • Enable online rooms, matchmaking, and multiplayer gameplay.
  • Save scores, progress, preferences, and reward status.
  • Improve game balance, reliability, and user experience.
  • Detect technical problems, abuse, fraud, or security risks.
  • Show advertisements and measure ad performance.
  • Process optional purchases through the app store.
  • Respond to support requests and user questions.
  • Comply with applicable legal requirements.

4. Advertising

CallBreak Go contains advertisements. Advertising providers, including Google AdMob, may collect or receive device identifiers, approximate location, app activity, ad interaction data, and technical information to provide, limit, personalize, and measure advertisements, subject to applicable consent and privacy settings.

You may be able to control personalized advertising through your device settings, Google account settings, or any consent options shown in the game.

Rewarded advertisements are optional. When available, you may choose to watch an advertisement in exchange for an in-game reward.

5. In-App Purchases

The game may offer optional in-app purchases. Payments are processed by Google Play or another authorized app-store provider. The developer does not directly receive or store your full payment-card information.

Purchase details, such as the purchased item, transaction confirmation, and entitlement status, may be processed to deliver and restore purchased content.

6. Third-Party Services

CallBreak Go may use third-party services for advertising, payments, game distribution, diagnostics, hosting, or online functionality. These providers process information under their own privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

CallBreak Go is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law. If a child uses the game, a parent or legal guardian should supervise the use of online features, advertisements, and purchases.

If you believe a child has provided personal information without appropriate consent, contact us so the matter can be reviewed.

8. Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the game, maintain records, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, enforce agreements, and meet legal obligations.

Some local gameplay data may remain on your device until you clear the app's data or uninstall the game. Information handled by third-party services is retained according to those providers' policies.

9. Security

Reasonable administrative and technical measures are used to protect information. However, no internet transmission, storage system, or mobile application can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or objection regarding certain personal information.

  • You can clear local game data through your device settings.
  • You can reset or limit your advertising identifier through device settings.
  • You can decline optional rewarded advertisements.
  • You can disable notifications through device settings.
  • You can request assistance by contacting the developer.

Identity verification may be required before fulfilling certain privacy requests.

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the game, legal requirements, or third-party services change. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
